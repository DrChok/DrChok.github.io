(R,R)-tartaric acid (S,S)-tartaric acid (R,S)-tartaric acid (S,R)-tartaric acid
There is an internal mirror plane in (R,S)-tartaric acid and (S,R)-tartaric acid, hence these two mirror images are the same molecule.
You can rotate the two molecules to see that they are indeed the same!
On the other hand, (R,R)-tartaric acid and (S,S)-tartaric acid are a pair of enantiomers, as the mirror images are not superposable.
Hence there are three stereoisomers altogether.
